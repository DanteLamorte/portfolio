YouTube Channel Analytics

Predicting video performance for Extra History

Data Science II · Coderhouse · 2025

7 Models
R² Optimized

Pulled the full video catalog of a major educational channel straight from the YouTube Data API v3 with pagination handling. Built features like short detection, weekend flag, title length, and publish hour; cleaned outliers via IQR. Used PLSR for feature selection, then tested 7 regression models with 5-fold cross-validation: Linear, Ridge, Lasso, ElasticNet, Decision Tree, Random Forest, and Gradient Boosting, with GridSearchCV for tuning. 02 / NLP + Deep Learning

World News Sentiment Analysis

r/worldnews · 2008 to 2016 · sentiment classification

Data Science III · Coderhouse · 2025

3 Models
LSTM Deep Layer

Full NLP pipeline on 8 years of Reddit world news headlines. Text cleaning covered lowercasing, punctuation removal, tokenization, stopword filtering, and lemmatization with custom exclusions. Used TextBlob for sentiment scoring and word cloud breakdowns by category. Then trained three classifiers: Bag of Words, TF-IDF with Logistic Regression, and a Bidirectional LSTM (Keras/TensorFlow), going from classical ML to deep learning to test whether word order actually moves the needle on sentiment classification.
